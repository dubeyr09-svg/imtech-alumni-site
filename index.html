Purpose & Scope Why the Alumni Association exists

The IMTECH Alumni Association is being set up as a non-profit, non-political body that connects former students, research fellows, faculty, and staff of the Institute of Microbial Technology (IMTECH), Chandigarh.

Its scope spans:

  • All degree, diploma, project, and post-doctoral alumni who spent time at IMTECH
  • Global participation ‚Äì India and international chapters (US, EU, UK, Asia, etc.)
  • Support to current IMTECH students, research fellows, and early-career scientists
Academic & industrial networking

Key Objectives What we aim to do together

For alumni
  • Maintain a secure & updated alumni database
  • Enable networking across batches, labs, and geographies
  • Facilitate peer-to-peer mentoring and collaborations
  • Celebrate alumni achievements and milestones
For current students & IMTECH
  • Offer mentoring circles, career talks, and mock interviews
  • Support internships, projects, and placement opportunities
  • Connect IMTECH labs to industry, startups, and funding opportunities
  • Support institute initiatives, endowments, and outreach
